I’m Ralts, born in the quiet forests of Hoenn, where I first developed a sensitivity to emotions and subtle psychic waves.

Fascinated by the unseen connections between minds, I later pursued studies in cognitive empathy and emotional intelligence at the Psychic Research Institute.

My research explored how psychic-type Pokémon perceive and process human emotions, and how this understanding can enhance communication and cooperation.

After contributing to several interdisciplinary projects across Sinnoh and Galar, I was invited to join Orange Academy.

Now, I teach “Emotion Science and Psychic Interaction,” helping students uncover the power of empathy, intuition, and mindful connection.
